Chocolate Factory never closer !

At the children’s Chocolate Factory little ones can join in a chocolate workshop where they choose to make a shape of their choice such as cats, bears, trains, dinosaurs, cars, planes, bunnies and at Christmas snowmen, stars, Xmas Trees and even father Christmas! They then create their own chocolate bar mixing milk and white chocolate decorating it with sweets and then have their own name piped in chocolate on the bar. Finally they make a marshmallow, chocolate and sweet creation of their own just in case they haven’t had enough chocolate they can have their faced painted and also squirts of it in their mouth!
